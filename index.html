  1. Drag the button above into your browser's bookmarks bar.

  3. Click the ⚡ Claude Usage bookmark. A tab will open here with your live quota.
What does it do? Is it safe?
The bookmarklet runs only when you click it, and only on the tab you clicked it in. On claude.ai, it reads your lastActiveOrg cookie value (an ID, not a secret) and calls claude.ai/api/organizations/<org>/usage using your existing login — exactly the same request claude.ai makes to render its own sidebar widget. The JSON response is base64-encoded into the URL hash of this page. Nothing is sent to any third-party server. Your Claude session cookie never leaves claude.ai.
